How you can get involved

Volunteering Opportunities:



https://humantraffickinghotline.org/en/get-involved human trafficking hotline is a great volunteer opportunity for those looking to get directly involved with stopping human/ child trafficking.

https://guardiangroup.org/volunteering-to-help-stop-child-sex-trafficking/ The PURSUIT team helps identify human trafficking online. You can join the PURSUIT team through their training and assessment course and personally help find and identify human trafficking on the internet.

https://ourrescue.org/join-the-fight/volunteerhttps://ourrescue.org/join-the-fight/volunteer The ourrescue organization powers many events to prevent and raise awareness on human trafficking. You can join their online community through their website and on Facebook.

Advocacy and Awareness


Child trafficking is a serious problem but you can help stop it by spreading awareness, signing petitions, and sharing facts on social media. These actions will help inform other people and push for stronger laws to protect children. You can find petitions to sign on non profit organizations. Many organizations working to combat child trafficking offer opportunities to sign petitions on their websites. One upcoming event you can participate in is the freedom walk for children, June 20th, 2025.
