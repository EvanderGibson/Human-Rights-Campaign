FAQ

What are good charities I can support in order to help fight child trafficking?

A good charity to support is Goya Cares. When asked to describe Goya Cares Bob Unanue, President & CEO of Goya Foods said "At the center of Goya Cares is a heart. Together, we must continue to fight and bring the child back to the center of our care and concern so that they may have hope and the opportunity to live in a world where their life is valued and their freedom is a reality".
What are the main motivations forChild trafficking?

Child trafficking is usually done for sexual or labor purposes and is very profitable.
What is child exploitation?

The act of using a child for someone else’s gain in a harmful or abusive way.
What is a Trafficker?

Traffickers are people who recruit, control, or sell children for sex. They are also those who pay for sex with a child and or those who facilitate the crime.
Who are common victims of child trafficking?

Traffickers often target vulnerable children, who experience instability, poverty, and lack parental supervision.
What can parents do to keep their children safe from child trafficking?

Parents can help protect their child from human trafficking by teaching them about safety and setting rules and boundaries for online use. Parents should also help their children recognize predatory activity.
What ages does the term youth refer to?

Defined by the United Nations “youth” are people between the ages of 15 and 24.
What is child sex trafficking?

According to federal law, child sex trafficking is defined as “commercial sexual exploitation of minors,” or “the prostitution of children” this means anyone who recruits, entices, harbors, transports, or solicits a person under 18 years of age, while knowing that the person is a minor and that the person will be forced to engage in a commercial sex act is engaging in child sex trafficking.
What does the US government do to prevent child trafficking?

The US uses many components to combat human trafficking while the FBI and DHS usually combat and prevent trafficking.
What is Branding?

Branding is a tattoo, marking, or carving placed upon a victim to identify them to a pimp, trafficker, gang.
What is a pimp?

A pimp is a person, in most cases men who control one or more sex workers and in turn take a cut of their pay.
What do the terms “john” “buyer” or “trick” mean?

These terms all refer to an individual who pays and or trades something valuable in exchange for sexual acts.
What’s a term for trafficking victims being traded?

Trafficking victims being exchanged for each other is known as trade up/trade down.
What is “Squaring Up”?

Squaring up refers to an attempt made by a victim to escape or exit prostitution.
How can you identify a victim of Child trafficking?

Possible signs to identify trafficking victims include a person being noticeably controlled by someone or signs that they are psychologically or physically abused.
